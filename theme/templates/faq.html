The objective of the web application is to give students a platform to share their notes between a study group.
Users can create a study group that others can visit.
Within the study groups, users can post notes related to the subject in the form of images or documents.
Users can also view what other users have posted. Others can rate and comment on the notes as a way of giving feedback or approval.
